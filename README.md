## Overview

Welcome to the **Sustainable Data Center Location Finder**! This project is designed to help users identify optimal locations for establishing data centers powered by renewable energy resources. It includes tools to calculate carbon emissions, assess energy efficiency, and offers visual insights through interactive maps.


## ğŸš€ Features

- **Interactive Map:** Explore potential locations and visualize their suitability.
- **Carbon Emission Calculator:** Estimate carbon emissions for selected locations.
- **Efficiency Calculator:** Evaluate the energy efficiency of proposed data centers.
- **Location Search:** Quickly find and filter locations based on your criteria.
- **Performance Insights:** Get a detailed analysis of energy efficiency.
